Statement

Yan-Lin CHEN is a new media art experimenter and audiovisual learner, currently residing in HsinChu, Taiwan. His works involve with interactive devices and real-time sound performance. He uses open source softwares to create audio-visual real-time performance. The works are dedicated to smooth combination of images and sounds created by computer algorithm as well as human perception with the universe and nature.
